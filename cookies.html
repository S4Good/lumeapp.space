Cookie Policy

This website uses cookies to provide you with the best possible user experience. This Cookie Policy explains what cookies are, how we use them, and how you can manage your preferences.

1. What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device (computer, tablet, smartphone) when you visit a website. They allow the website to recognize your device and store information about your preferences and behavior.

2. Types of cookies we use

Technically Necessary Cookies

These cookies are essential for the basic functionality of the website. They enable core features such as navigation, security, and accessibility. These cookies are set without requiring your consent, as they are necessary to provide the service you requested.

Optional/Analytics Cookies

Optional cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our website. We only set these cookies with your explicit consent. These may include analytics, performance monitoring, and user preference tracking.

3. How to manage cookies

You can control which cookies are set on your device through:

  • Our cookie consent banner: When you first visit, you can choose to accept all cookies or only necessary ones.
  • Browser settings: You can configure your browser to refuse all cookies or to alert you when a cookie is being set.
  • Cookie management tools: Various third-party services allow you to manage and delete cookies across multiple websites.

4. Cookie consent

We respect your privacy and only set optional cookies when you have explicitly consented to them. Your consent preference is stored locally in your browser using the key lume_cookie_consent so we remember your choice on future visits.

5. Data processing

Your data is processed on the basis of Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR (consent) and Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR (legitimate interest in providing a functioning website). We do not share your data with third parties without your consent, except where required by law.
