Cookie Policy

This website uses technically necessary storage and, with your consent, optional cookies (e.g. analytics) to improve the service.

1. What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device. They contain information that can be read when you visit the website again.

2. Types of cookies

2.1 Technically necessary cookies

These cookies are required for the basic functionality of the website, such as storing your cookie preferences. Without them, the site cannot function properly.

2.2 Optional cookies (e.g. analytics)

Optional cookies are only set after your explicit consent. These may include analytics tools that help us understand how the website is used.

3. Legal basis

Technically necessary cookies are processed on the basis of Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR (legitimate interest in operating a functional website). Optional cookies are processed exclusively on the basis of your consent in accordance with Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR.

4. Managing your cookie preferences

You can configure your browser to restrict or completely block cookies. Cookies already stored on your device can be deleted at any time via your browser settings.

To change your selection in our cookie banner, please delete the stored cookies in your browser and reload the website.
